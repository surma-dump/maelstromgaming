Team Imagine

Team Imagine is a League of Legends team currently participanting in the North American Challenger Series. The team is made up of recognizable talent like former LCS players Mancloud and Steelback as well as high profile challenger talent such as Moon, Baby, Chunkyfresh and Wolfe.


Our roster is backed up by veteran staff with extensive background in esports ranging from ex pro-players turned analysts to full time proven coaches.
